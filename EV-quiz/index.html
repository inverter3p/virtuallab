EV Tutorial Summary

A collection of modules covering after midterm topics.

Motor & Control

PMSM Speed Control

A review of control strategies for Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motors in electric drivetrains.
Motor & Control

Motor Encoders

An overview of incremental and absolute encoders for motion feedback in closed-loop systems.
Powertrain Dynamics

Regenerative Braking

An analysis of the principles and control methods for recapturing kinetic energy during vehicle deceleration.
Powertrain Dynamics

Power Inverter

Examines the inverter's role, comparing SPWM and SVPWM modulation and key power electronic components.
Energy Storage

EV Battery: Part 1

Introduces fundamental concepts of Li-ion batteries, including core terminology, cell chemistries, and pack construction.
Energy Storage

EV Battery: Part 2

Covers advanced battery characteristics, performance metrics, and the functions of a Battery Management System (BMS).
Infrastructure

EV Charging Technology

A quick look at charging infrastructure, from AC/DC architectures to communication protocols and global standards.
Infrastructure

V2X Technology

An exploration of Vehicle-to-Everything concepts, bidirectional charging, and the role of EVs in grid services.
